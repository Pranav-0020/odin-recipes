Lemon Juice Recipe

Description

The lemon is a very healthy fruit that is loaded with vitamin C and fiber.Lemon juice is a valuable source of nutrients called flavonoids. These are antioxidants, which help protect cells from damage that could lead to cancer.

Ingredients

  • Lemon - 2 medium size
  • Ice Cubes - 2 cups
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 3 tbsp or to taste
  • Salt - Â½ tsp

Instructions for making lemon juice:

  1. Take a blender and squeeze in the lemon juice.
  2. Cut a small piece of lemon peel. You have to add it to the blender
  3. Now add in salt, sugar, ice cubes and ice water.
  4. Now blend this for 30 seconds till everything is well blundered.
  5. Pour into serving glasses and serve immediately.
